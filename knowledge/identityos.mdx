IdentityOS is the foundation of how Pressmaster understands you. Your Content Twin, brand voice, and brand guide are unified into a single system — one place to define who you are, how you think, and how you communicate. Every agent and tool in Pressmaster draws from it automatically, so you never have to re-explain yourself.

## Your Content Twin

Your Content Twin is an evolving map of how you think — your ideas, stories, values, patterns, and lived experience. It gets sharper with every training session, upload, and interaction, and makes content feel genuinely like you rather than generic AI output.

### Explore your Twin Map

Your Twin is visualized as an interactive map built around your identity, connecting your thinking across categories including:

![Content Twin mind map showing knowledge categories radiating from your name](/images/twin-map.png)

- Stories & Anecdotes
- Frameworks & Mental Models
- Decision Patterns
- Hooks & Content Seeds
- Expertise & Domains
- Values & Principles
- Reasoning Style
- Identity & Emotion
- Proof Points & Evidence
- Vision & Predictions
- Relationships & Influences
- Opinions & Contrarian Takes
- Gaps & Tensions
- Problems & Solutions

You can zoom, scroll, and click through nodes to explore how the Twin understands your thinking. To remove a specific connection, click directly on it and delete it.

### Ways to train your Twin

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Guided trainings" icon="graduation-cap">
    Structured, topic-specific interviews that strengthen different dimensions of your identity and expertise. Sessions include Basic Interview, Identity & Values, Story & Purpose, Expertise & Knowledge, Communication & Voice, and Leadership & Execution. Complete them in any order.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Upload material" icon="upload">
    Paste plain text, notes, articles, or research directly into the Twin. Upload files (PDF, DOC, slides) or add links — Pressmaster auto-scrapes linked pages and extracts insights into your knowledge map.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Twin Chat" icon="message-circle">
    Go beyond reference — explore, strategize, and execute with your Twin via chat. It can save ideas, fire creation processes, and build full plans. To make it retain something, explicitly say: "Memorize this."
  </Card>
  <Card title="Streaming Training" icon="zap">
    Enable Streaming Training in **Settings** to let the Twin automatically learn from your interviews, journals, and conversations. When disabled, the Twin learns only from what you manually upload.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Twin IQ Score

Your **Twin IQ Score** measures how well-trained and reliable your Twin is. The score ranges from 0 to 999.

![Twin IQ Score gauge showing score ranges from Low to Excellent](/images/twin-iq-score.png)

| Range | Level |
|---|---|
| 0–49 | Low |
| 50–99 | Average |
| 100–149 | Good |
| 150–200 | Excellent |

You need a score of **100 or above** to use your Twin reliably for content creation. There is no upper limit — the more data you add, the higher and more precise your score becomes.

### Using your Twin in content creation

Whenever you create content, enable your Twin by clicking the **brain icon** next to the agent. When active, the Twin makes your voice more precise, your opinions consistent, and your expertise clear. The editor also has direct Twin access — so every edit stays in your voice without re-entering context.

## Brand Guide, Voice Profiles & Creative Director

Your brand guidelines tell Pressmaster *what* to say, *how* to say it, and *how it should look*.

![IdentityOS Brand Guide showing Brand Foundation, Voice Profiles, and Creative Director setup](/images/brand-guide.png)

| | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Brand Foundation** | Your mission, target audience, positioning, and strategic principles |
| **Voice Profiles** | Your writing style, tone, sentence structure, and rhythm — manage different profiles for different contexts and platforms |
| **Creative Director** | Your visual style — guides AI-generated images to match your brand's aesthetic, colors, and design language |

### Setting up your Brand Foundation

Open **IdentityOS** and click **Set up my brand rules**. Choose how you want to build it:

![Brand Foundation setup showing Advanced Discovery, Do It Yourself, and Quick Mode options](/images/brand-foundation-setup.png)

- **Advanced Discovery** *(Recommended)* — a guided 5–10 minute interview that builds a complete, structured understanding of your company and brand identity
- **Do It Yourself** — define your brand identity manually by completing each section yourself; upload existing materials or write everything from scratch
- **Quick Mode** — enter your website URL and Pressmaster instantly generates a full brand guide using automatic extraction; ideal for getting started in minutes

### Setting up a Voice Profile

Open **IdentityOS** and click **Create voice profile**. Choose how you want to build it:

![Voice Profile setup showing Advanced Discovery, Do It Yourself, and Quick Mode options](/images/voice-profile-setup.png)

- **Advanced Discovery** *(Recommended)* — a 5–10 minute interview that captures how you think and speak, then turns it into your brand voice
- **Do It Yourself** — add your own rules, tone guidelines, and templates to manually shape your AI's writing style
- **Quick Mode** — answer a few quick questions to automatically generate your brand voice in just minutes

### Multiple Voice Profiles

| Plan | Voice Profiles |
|---|---|
| Starter | 1 |
| Pro | 2 |
| Leader | Unlimited |
| Agency plans | Unlimited |

### Creative Director

Your Creative Director defines your visual identity — guiding how AI-generated images match your brand's aesthetic, colors, and design language. Set it up once and every visual Pressmaster creates stays on-brand without you having to specify style details each time.

Click **Create creative director** in the Brand Guide section and choose how you want to define it:

![Creative Director setup showing Upload files, Do It Yourself, and Quick Mode options](/images/creative-director-setup.png)

- **Upload files** *(Recommended)* — upload images, PDFs, or design samples; Pressmaster analyzes your colors, typography, and layouts
- **Do It Yourself** — write a prompt describing your visual style and guidelines
- **Quick Mode** — enter a website URL and instantly generate creative direction using automatic extraction; ideal for matching an existing brand's style

## MCP for Identity — Twin Export

Connect your Twin and identity to any external AI tool via MCP (Model Context Protocol). Your identity travels with you across your entire AI stack — so you can use your Pressmaster Twin in Claude, Cursor, or any other MCP-compatible tool.

Your identity becomes portable. Use your Twin wherever you work.

## Language settings

Pressmaster generates content in the language set in **Settings > Account Settings > Default Language**. Supported languages include UK English, American English, and German.

<Tip>
  Your Brand Guide shows "in progress" if you haven't answered every question — that's fine. Pressmaster uses whatever information you've already provided. Fill in more detail over time for increasingly personalized results.
</Tip>
