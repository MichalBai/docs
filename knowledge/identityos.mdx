IdentityOS is the foundation of how Pressmaster understands you. It combines two things: your **Content Twin** — an evolving map of how you think — and your **brand guidelines**, which capture your voice, tone, and positioning. Together, they ensure every piece of content Pressmaster generates is accurate to your thinking and consistent with your brand.

## Your Content Twin

Your Content Twin maps your ideas, stories, values, patterns, and lived experience. Pressmaster uses it to create content that genuinely sounds like you — drawing on your real reasoning and expertise rather than generic patterns. The Twin gets sharper with every training session, upload, and interaction.

### Explore your Twin Map

Your Twin is visualized as an interactive map built around your identity. It shows how your thoughts connect across categories including:

- Stories & Anecdotes
- Frameworks & Mental Models
- Decision Patterns
- Hooks & Content Seeds
- Expertise & Domains
- Values & Principles
- Reasoning Style
- Identity & Emotion
- Proof Points & Evidence
- Vision & Predictions
- Relationships & Influences
- Opinions & Contrarian Takes
- Gaps & Tensions
- Problems & Solutions

You can zoom, scroll, and click through nodes to explore how the Twin understands your thinking. To remove a specific connection, click directly on it and delete it.

### Ways to train your Twin

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Guided trainings" icon="graduation-cap">
    Structured, topic-specific interviews that strengthen different dimensions of your identity and expertise. Available sessions include Basic Interview, Identity & Values, Story & Purpose, Expertise & Knowledge, Communication & Voice, and Leadership & Execution. Complete them in any order.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Upload material" icon="upload">
    Paste plain text, notes, articles, or research directly into the Twin. Upload files (PDF, DOC, slides) or add links — Pressmaster auto-scrapes linked pages. The Twin extracts insights and adds them to your knowledge map.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Twin Chat" icon="message-circle">
    Use the Twin's dedicated chat to explore ideas, test its reasoning, expand existing insights, and train it through conversation. To make the Twin retain what you say in chat, explicitly tell it: "Memorize this."
  </Card>
  <Card title="Streaming Training" icon="zap">
    Enable Streaming Training in **Settings** to let the Twin automatically learn from your interviews, journals, and conversations. When disabled, the Twin learns only from what you manually upload or ask it to remember.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Twin IQ Score

Your **Twin IQ Score** measures how well-trained and reliable your Twin has become. The score ranges from 0 to 999 — the higher it is, the better your Twin performs when generating content.

| Range | Level |
|---|---|
| 0–49 | Low |
| 50–99 | Average |
| 100–149 | Good |
| 150–200 | Excellent |

You need a score of **100 or above** to use your Twin reliably for content creation. There is no upper limit — the more data you add, the higher and more precise your score becomes.

### Using your Twin in content creation

Whenever you create content, enable your Twin by clicking the **brain icon** next to the agent. When active, the Twin makes your voice more precise, your opinions consistent, and your expertise clear.

<Note>
  To make the Twin memorize something said in chat, explicitly tell it: "Memorize this." Otherwise it treats the message as temporary conversation.
</Note>

## Brand Guide & Brand Voice

Your brand guidelines tell Pressmaster *what* to say and *how* to say it. They work together with your Twin to shape every piece of content.

| | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Brand Guide** | Your mission, target audience, positioning, and company overview — the strategic context Pressmaster uses to understand your brand |
| **Brand Voice** | Your writing style and tone — the tactical voice Pressmaster applies when generating content |

### Creating a Brand Voice

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings > Brand Voice">
    Open **Settings** from the left menu, then select **Brand Guides**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Add Brand Voice'">
    You'll find this option in the Brand Voice section, after completing your general Brand Guide.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide a writing sample">
    Paste a piece of content you've written — a social post, article, or any text that sounds like you. If you don't have an existing sample, you can paste a post from a creator whose style you want to use as a starting framework.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start the analysis">
    Click **Start Analyzing**. Pressmaster examines your tone, sentence structure, narrative approach, and other communication patterns.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and customize the result">
    Review the generated Brand Voice profile. Adjust any detected attributes and add your own custom instructions. For improved formatting accuracy, scroll to the bottom and add: "Always follow the following structure" — then paste a complete example post below it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name and save your Brand Voice">
    Give the profile a descriptive name (for example: "LinkedIn posts" or "Long-form articles"), then click **Apply Brand Voice** to save it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Multiple Brand Voices

You can create separate Brand Voice profiles for different content types or clients.

| Plan | Brand Voices |
|---|---|
| Starter | 1 |
| Pro | 2 |
| Leader | Unlimited |
| Agency plans | Unlimited |

### Language settings

Pressmaster generates content in the language you set in **Settings > Account Settings > Default Language**. Supported languages currently include UK English, American English, and German.

<Tip>
  Your Brand Guide shows "in progress" if you haven't answered every question — that's fine. Pressmaster uses whatever information you've already provided. Fill in more detail over time for increasingly personalized results.
</Tip>
