Saved Ideas lets you capture inspiration the moment it strikes and act on it immediately. Save any idea, link, video, file, or text note — then launch directly into Brainstorm, Instant Creation, Plan Ahead, or an Interview without losing momentum.

## What you can save

- **YouTube videos** — save video URLs and generate summaries for content repurposing
- **Articles and links** — save any web URL; Pressmaster generates a concise, intelligent summary
- **Podcasts** — save podcast links and extract key ideas
- **Files and images** — upload documents or images directly
- **Text notes** — jot down insights, quotes, or meeting highlights
- **Ideas from chat** — save any idea or plan directly from a Brainstorm or chat session

When adding a link, provide an optional focus note to direct Pressmaster's attention to a specific angle. Leave it blank for a general summary.

## From idea to content in one step

Once saved, Pressmaster generates **15 personalized content angles** based on the idea and your brand guide. From any angle, jump directly into:

- **Brainstorm** — explore and develop the idea through chat or interview
- **Instant Creation** — generate a ready-to-post article or social post immediately
- **Plan Ahead** — feed it into a broader content plan
- **Interview** — run a focused interview on the topic

Every idea is one step from becoming content. No idle saves.

## Using the mobile app

Download the Pressmaster mobile app to save ideas from anywhere — commutes, meetings, or while reading. Everything syncs and is ready for content creation whenever you are.
