Saved Ideas lets you capture ideas the moment they strike — whether you're on your commute, browsing an article, or watching a video. Saved content becomes raw material for your Content Twin and creation flows, turning everyday inspiration into a steady pipeline of on-brand content.

## What you can save

- **YouTube videos** — save video URLs and summarize them for content repurposing
- **Articles and links** — save any web URL; Pressmaster generates a concise, intelligent summary
- **Podcasts** — save podcast links and extract key ideas with a focused summary
- **Files and images** — upload documents or images directly
- **Text notes** — jot down insights, quotes, or meeting highlights

When adding a link, you can provide an optional focus note to tell Pressmaster what angle or section to pay attention to. Leave it blank for a general summary.

## How saved content is used

Once you have a summary, Pressmaster generates **15 personalized content angles** based on your saved content and brand guide. From any of those ideas you can immediately:

- **Brainstorm** — Explore and develop the idea through chat or interview
- **Create instantly** — Generate a ready-to-post article or social post
- **Plan ahead** — Feed it into a broader content plan

All content is prefilled and editable, tailored to your tone. Every saved idea is stored in your library, where you can combine multiple items, merge a saved idea with a past interview, or cross-reference with recently generated posts.

Saved content also feeds into your Content Twin's knowledge base and serves as source material for Plan Ahead.

## Using the mobile app

Download the Pressmaster mobile app to save ideas from anywhere without opening a laptop. Use the app to save links, take screenshots, and summarize content on the go — everything syncs and is ready for content creation whenever you are.

  Use the mobile app to save voice notes and links during your commute. Pressmaster transcribes and stores them automatically, ready to become content.
</Tip>
