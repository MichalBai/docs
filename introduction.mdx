## How Pressmaster works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Teach Pressmaster your brand">
    Provide your website URL or paste a brand overview. In 45 seconds, Pressmaster builds a comprehensive brand guide — capturing your voice, mission, audience, and tone.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your channels">
    Link your social media accounts (LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, Facebook, Bluesky) and your blog (WordPress, Webflow, Wix, Ghost). Pressmaster publishes directly to all of them.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Discover trending topics">
    Your TrendMaster dashboard scans 500,000+ sources daily to surface the most relevant topics in your industry, weeks before they peak.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create and publish content">
    Run a 5-minute interview, activate the Agent-Team, or use Pin Your Thoughts to capture ideas. Pressmaster turns your input into polished articles, social posts, and press releases — ready to publish with one click.
  </Step>
</Steps>
