## How Pressmaster works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set up your identity">
    Go to [IdentityOS](/knowledge/identityos) and set up your Brand Guide, Brand Voice, and Content Twin. Pressmaster uses this to understand who you are, how you write, and what you stand for — so every piece of content it creates is accurate to your thinking and consistent with your brand.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your channels">
    Link your social media accounts (LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, Facebook, Bluesky) and your blog (WordPress, Webflow, Wix, Ghost). Pressmaster publishes directly to all of them.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate your Super Agent">
    Turn on your Super Agent in [Agent Studio](/agent-studio/overview). Once active, it works in the background — monitoring signals, planning content, and executing tasks so you don't have to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Train your Content Twin">
    Run guided training sessions, upload your existing content, or use Twin Chat to build up your Twin's knowledge. Aim for a Twin IQ Score of 100 or above for the best content quality.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create and publish content">
    Use [Find Ideas](/find-ideas/overview) to discover what's trending in your industry, then create through [Explore Ideas](/creating-content/explore-ideas), [Instant Creation](/creating-content/instant-creation), or [Plan Ahead](/creating-content/plan-ahead). All content lands in your [Content Library](/content-library/overview) ready to review and publish.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What you can create

- **Social media posts** — platform-specific content for LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, Facebook, and Bluesky
- **Articles** — long-form thought leadership with SEO optimization, data enrichment, and fact-checking
- **Press releases** — distributed to 400+ publications including Business Insider and Yahoo Finance
- **Infographics & carousels** — visual content formats (Pro plan and above)
