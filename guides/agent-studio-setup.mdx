In this video, I walk you through the full setup process for Agent Studio inside Pressmaster.ai.

Agent Studio lets you build AI agents that can support your content workflow, connect with your tools, and work across channels like WhatsApp, Telegram, Slack, email, and more. Instead of manually managing every step of your thought leadership process, you can create agents that help you plan, organize, execute, and automate parts of your content system.

In this tutorial, you'll learn how Agent Studio works, how to set up your first agent, which settings matter, and the best practices for building useful AI agents inside Pressmaster.ai.
