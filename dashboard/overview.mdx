The Dashboard is a clean home screen designed to orient you immediately and get you into action fast. It surfaces what needs your attention, shows your latest performance data, and puts every creation flow one click away.

## What's on your Dashboard

### Today's Brief

A personalized summary generated each day based on your latest performance signals. It highlights BPI changes, flags shifts in engagement, and tells you what to prioritize — so you land and launch without having to figure out where to start.

### Quick starters

Jump directly into any creation flow:

- **Brainstorm** — explore and develop ideas through chat or interview
- **Interview** — launch an interview on any topic instantly, in text, voice, or video
- **Instant Creation** — create a single ready-to-post piece right now
- **Plan Ahead** — build a full content plan for the week or month

### Your Performance

A snapshot of your current metrics:

- **Impressions** — total content views across all channels
- **Engagement Rate** — percentage of viewers who interacted
- **Brand Popularity Index (BPI)** — your overall brand authority score
- **Total Followers** — combined audience across connected platforms

Each metric shows the percentage change from the previous week. See [Performance](/performance/overview) for a deeper breakdown.

### Upcoming Content

A preview of your next scheduled posts — title, platform(s), and publish time. Click any item to open it in the editor or reschedule.

### Notifications

The Dashboard auto-updates with notifications as they become relevant — including performance reviews, agent activity updates, and content approvals that need your attention.

## Getting the most from your Dashboard

The Dashboard reflects the quality of your setup. The more complete your [IdentityOS](/knowledge/identityos) and the more channels you have connected, the more accurate and useful your daily brief will be.

<Tip>
  Check Today's Brief each morning — it's generated from your latest performance data and gives you a clear starting point for your content work.
</Tip>
