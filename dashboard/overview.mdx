The Dashboard is the first thing you see when you open Pressmaster. It gives you a live snapshot of your brand performance, surfaces what needs your attention today, and puts your most-used creation flows one click away.

## What's on your Dashboard

### Today's Brief

At the top of the Dashboard, Pressmaster generates a **Today's Brief** — a short, personalized summary of your current performance and what to prioritize. It highlights changes in your BPI, flags any performance shifts, and recommends next steps.

### Your Performance

A summary of your key metrics for the current period:

- **Impressions** — total content views across channels
- **Engagement Rate** — percentage of viewers who interacted
- **Brand Popularity Index (BPI)** — your overall brand authority score
- **Total Followers** — combined audience across connected platforms

Each metric shows the percentage change from the previous week. See [Performance](/performance/overview) for a deeper breakdown.

### Quick starters

Jump directly into a creation flow from the Dashboard:

- **Brainstorm** — explore and develop an idea through chat or interview
- **Interview** — run a quick interview on a topic and turn it into content
- **Instant Creation** — create a single ready-to-post piece right now
- **Plan Ahead** — build a full content plan for the week or month ahead

### Upcoming Content

A preview of your next scheduled posts — showing title, platform(s), and publish time. Click any item to open it in the editor or reschedule it.

## Getting the most from your Dashboard

The Dashboard reflects the quality of your setup. The more complete your [IdentityOS](/knowledge/identityos) — your Content Twin, Brand Guide, and Brand Voice — and the more channels you have connected, the more accurate and useful your performance data and Today's Brief will be.

<Tip>
  Check Today's Brief each morning. It's generated based on your latest performance data and gives you a clear starting point for your content work that day.
</Tip>
