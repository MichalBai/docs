Pressmaster's built-in editor handles every content type in one place — social posts, articles, newsletters, and video scripts. Once your content is generated, the editor is where you refine it, collaborate on it, and send it live.

## AI-assisted editing

**Inline AI bar** — select any text and click the AI button to rephrase, rewrite, adjust tone, or get suggestions. The result inserts instantly.

**Chat with AI** — open the Chat with AI panel to refine your content through conversation. Ask questions, request changes, or give direction — the agent edits your draft iteratively without regenerating the whole piece.

**Twin collaboration** — the editor has direct access to your Content Twin. Every edit stays consistent with your voice, your opinions, and your expertise — without re-entering any context. Your Twin is always in the room.

## Optimization tools

Ask the chat agent to:

- **SEO optimize** — structure content for target keywords and readability
- **Humanize** — reduce AI patterns and make the writing feel more natural
- **Improve readability** — simplify structure, shorten sentences, improve flow
- **Identify gaps** — surface missing angles or unsupported claims

The agent creates a multi-step plan and executes it automatically. Enable **auto-accept** to let it apply changes without interruption, or review each one manually.

## Cross-channel editing

When creating for multiple platforms, edit drafts for all 8+ channels simultaneously from the same editor. Enable **Apply to All** and changes propagate instantly across every format — or fine-tune each channel version individually.

## Images and media

- Click the image box at the top to upload a cover image
- Highlight a location in the body and select **Image** to insert mid-content
- Paste images directly into the editor
- Generate on-brand visuals — stock images, infographics, quote visuals, or cinematic carousels — without leaving the editor

## Sharing for feedback

Generate a **Feedback Link** to share your draft with colleagues, clients, or partners. Reviewers can highlight specific text and leave comments directly on the draft — no account required. Agency users get a white-labeled version of the feedback link for client-facing collaboration.

## Publishing from the editor

Once you're done, publish directly to your connected destinations:

- Social channels (LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, Facebook, Bluesky)
- Your connected blog (WordPress, Webflow, Wix, Ghost)
- Pressmaster Newsroom
- Marketplace (press release distribution)

Choose to publish immediately or schedule for a specific date and time.

<Warning>
  If you select the Marketplace as your publishing destination, the content will not be sent to your connected blog. Set your destination before you start writing.
</Warning>

<Tip>
  To change the tone of a section, select the text and use the inline AI bar to rephrase or set a new tone on the spot — no need to open the full chat panel.
</Tip>
