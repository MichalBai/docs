Every piece of content in Pressmaster starts from the **+ Create** button. From there, you choose one of three modes depending on what you want to accomplish.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Brainstorm" icon="lightbulb" href="/creating-content/brainstorm">
    Explore ideas through chat or interview. Best when you want to think through a topic, research it, and develop it before committing to a format.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Instant Creation" icon="bolt" href="/creating-content/instant-creation">
    Drop in an idea and get a ready-to-post piece of content. Best for creating a single article, social post, or press release quickly.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Plan Ahead" icon="calendar" href="/creating-content/plan-ahead">
    Build a full content plan. Best when you want to create multiple pieces at once — either by letting Pressmaster take the wheel, customizing your own plan, or repurposing existing content.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Which mode should you use?

| If you want to… | Use |
|---|---|
| Think through an idea before creating | Brainstorm |
| Create one piece of content right now | Instant Creation |
| Plan and create a week or more of content | Plan Ahead |
| Repurpose an existing article, video, or file | Plan Ahead → Repurpose Content |

## What happens after creation

All content you create — whether drafted, scheduled, or published — lives in your [Content Library](/content-library/overview). From there you can review, approve, reschedule, or publish any piece.

Scheduled content also appears in your [Calendar](/calendar/overview), giving you a complete view of what's going out and when.
