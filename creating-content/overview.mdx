Every piece of content in Pressmaster starts from the **+ Create** button. From there, you choose a mode based on where you are and what you want to accomplish. All modes share a unified editor and connect to the same creation flow — from idea to publish in one unbroken session.

## Start with an interview

Every creation mode can begin with an interview. Launch one instantly on any topic — no planning or setup required. Choose your format:

- **Text** — answer written questions at your own pace
- **Voice** — speak naturally, Pressmaster transcribes and extracts key ideas
- **Video** — talk to a virtual AI journalist on camera; auto-generate written posts and video clips from the session

You can switch between text, voice, and video mid-interview without losing context.

## Which mode should you use?

| If you want to… | Use |
|---|---|
| Think through an idea before creating | Brainstorm |
| Create one piece of content right now | Instant Creation |
| Plan and create a week or more of content | Plan Ahead |
| Repurpose an existing article, video, or file | Plan Ahead → Repurpose Content |
| Publish a quick post from inside a chat | Brainstorm → In-Chat Publishing |

## What happens after creation

All content you create — whether drafted, scheduled, or published — lands in your [Content Library](/content-library/overview). From there you can review, approve, reschedule, or publish any piece. Scheduled content also appears in your [Calendar](/calendar/overview).
