Pressmaster creates platform-specific social media posts optimized for each channel's format and algorithm. Write once with your brand voice, and publish across LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, Facebook, and Bluesky — individually or all at once.

## Supported post types

- Long-form LinkedIn posts
- Twitter/X threads
- Instagram captions
- Facebook posts
- Bluesky threads

## Create a social post

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Social Suite">
    From your dashboard, click **Social Suite**, then select **AI Post**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your topic or source">
    Share your own idea, select a suggestion from TrendMaster, or pull from your content library — including previous posts, existing articles, and past interviews. You can also paste text directly into the idea field to repurpose any content.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your post settings">
    Set your narrative style, brand voice, theme and style, and post length. Enable **Hook and Angle Simulation** to let Pressmaster test multiple opening hooks and recommend the highest-performing option before generating your post.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your target platform(s)">
    Choose one or more platforms: LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, Facebook, or Bluesky. Pressmaster generates a native version for each channel — not a single post copied across all of them.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and refine">
    Once the posts are generated, review each one in the editor. Use **Chat with AI** to adjust tone, rephrase sections, or apply quick AI prompts. You can also add internal comments or share a review link with teammates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add images (optional)">
    Upload your own images or videos, or use Auto Image Generation to create visuals that match your content.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish immediately or schedule">
    Click **Publish** to post across all selected channels now, or schedule for a later time. All scheduled posts appear in your content calendar on the dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Scheduling

Posts scheduled through Pressmaster appear in your content calendar. To reschedule, open the calendar, click the post, and select a new date. To cancel a scheduled post, open the post in the calendar and click **Cancel Post**.

<Tip>
  Add links in comments rather than in the post body. Links in posts reduce reach due to platform algorithms.
</Tip>

## Platform notes

**LinkedIn** — Pressmaster supports LinkedIn posts only. LinkedIn's API does not allow publishing LinkedIn articles, so article publishing is not available through Pressmaster.

**One account per platform per workspace** — Each workspace supports one connected social account per platform. If you manage multiple accounts on the same platform, use separate workspaces for each.

## Repurposing content into social posts

Turn any published Pressmaster article into social posts by selecting **New AI Post** and choosing **Use Existing Article as Reference**. If the article was created outside of Pressmaster, copy and paste it into the idea field instead. You can also use saved **Pin Your Thoughts** entries as source material for new posts.
