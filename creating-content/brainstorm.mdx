Brainstorm is a dedicated mode for thinking before doing. Use it to explore a topic, research angles, run an interview, analyze your performance, or develop a full content strategy — then deploy it to the creation flow without leaving the session.

## When to use Brainstorm

- You have a topic in mind but haven't shaped it into a clear angle yet
- You want to run an interview before writing
- You want to analyze your published performance and figure out what to do next
- You want to explore multiple angles before committing to a plan

## How it works

Open Brainstorm from the **+ Create** menu. You'll enter a chat interface where you can:

- **Start with an idea** — type what's on your mind and Pressmaster will help you explore and develop it
- **Run an interview** — let Pressmaster ask you targeted questions to extract your best thinking on any topic
- **Analyze and iterate** — chat with your published content performance data, identify gaps, find opportunities, and plan your next move
- **Save ideas** — save any idea, draft, or plan directly to your content queue from within the session so nothing gets lost

## Interview styles

When running an interview inside Brainstorm:

- **Adaptive** — questions are asked one at a time, each building on your previous answer
- **Conversational** — a free-flowing dialogue, like talking to a journalist
- **Voice** — speak your answers; Pressmaster transcribes and extracts your best ideas
- **Video** — talk to a virtual AI journalist on camera and auto-generate written content and clips

You can switch between text, voice, and video mid-interview without losing any context.

## Brainstorm → Creation handover

Any plan, idea, or strategy you develop in Brainstorm can be handed off to the creation agents with one click. The gap between thinking and doing disappears — strategize and execute in the same session.

- Send a single idea to **Instant Creation** for immediate publishing
- Send a full plan to **Plan Ahead** to build out a content calendar
- **Publish directly from chat** — create and publish LinkedIn posts and other channel content without switching modes

## Analyse performance and iterate

Chat with all your published content — posts, KPIs, engagement data — directly inside Brainstorm. Identify what's working, spot gaps, and deploy new plans without leaving the session.

<Tip>
  If your Content Twin has a score above 100, Brainstorm sessions draw on your existing thinking — making the output feel more like you from the first message.
</Tip>
