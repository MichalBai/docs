Plan Ahead is Pressmaster's multi-agent creation mode that plans, writes, and schedules multiple pieces of content on your behalf. Choose how much control you want to hand over, and the agent handles the rest.

<Warning>
  Set up your IdentityOS — including your Brand Guide, Brand Voice, and Content Twin — before running Plan Ahead. This significantly improves output quality.
</Warning>

## Three modes

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Just Handle It For Me">
    The fully autonomous option. Pressmaster acts as your strategist, builds a content plan from scratch, and creates everything without you having to make decisions along the way.

    **What the agent draws on:**

    - Your Audience & Positioning
    - Your Saved Ideas
    - Your Content Twin
    - Your trends from Find Ideas
    - Your recent performance signals

    **What the agent does:**

    - Generates a calibrated 7–30 day content plan
    - Mixes Growth, Trust, and Conversion angles
    - Creates original posts based on your identity and market movement
    - Ensures narrative consistency across all formats

    Best when you want the fastest path to a full content calendar.

  </Tab>
  <Tab title="I'll Customize My Plan">
    A collaborative mode where you direct the strategy. Choose your topics, channels, schedule, and the agent executes within those parameters.

    **What you control:**

    - Topics and angles to cover
    - Which channels to publish on
    - Publishing schedule and cadence

    **What the agent does:**

    - Builds the content plan within your specifications
    - Creates posts based on your identity and voice
    - Schedules everything according to your chosen timeline

    Best when you have a clear direction and want to stay in control of what gets created.

  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Repurpose Content">
    Use this mode when you already have existing content and want Pressmaster to break it down into a structured series.

    **What you can add:**

    - Files (PDF, DOC, slides, notes)
    - Links
    - YouTube videos
    - Raw text
    - Voice notes

    **What the agent does:**

    - Extracts the strongest ideas from your material
    - Identifies key angles and patterns
    - Converts everything into multiple posts or a full content series
    - Rewrites all content in your Brand Voice

    Best when you have a book, talk, article, or any long-form material you want to turn into a content engine.

  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## The Plan Ahead workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your mode">
    Select **Just Handle It For Me**, **I'll Customize My Plan**, or **Repurpose Content** based on your starting point.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review content ideas">
    The agent generates structured content ideas based on your brand, voice, and selected mode. From here you can:

    - **Iterate via chat** — ask the agent to expand, sharpen, adjust tone, or explore alternatives
    - **Select specific topics** — highlight ideas to anchor the next steps
    - **Approve all** — move forward with the full set

  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the calendar">
    The agent generates a proposed publishing schedule. Open each content piece to review its topic, angle, positioning, and purpose. Adjust Brand Voice, format, tone, length, and narrative on a per-item basis before moving on.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the interview (optional)">
    For each planned topic, choose how the agent collects your insights:

    - **Text interview** — answer short, focused questions directly in chat
    - **Voice interview** — speak naturally while the agent transcribes and extracts your best ideas
    - **Skip** — if your Content Twin is well-trained, skip entirely and let the agent generate from your existing knowledge base

  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and publish">
    Once the flow is complete, all content appears in your **Content Library** and **Calendar** — scheduled according to the proposed plan. Approve, edit, reschedule, or publish any piece from there.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  If your Content Twin has a score above 100, you can skip the interview step entirely — Plan Ahead will generate content from your existing knowledge base.
</Tip>
