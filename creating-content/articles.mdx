Pressmaster transforms your expertise into long-form articles with automatic data enrichment, real-time fact-checking, and SEO optimization. Start with a short AI interview or import existing content, and Pressmaster writes a publication-ready piece in your voice.

## Two ways to create articles

**AI Interview** — Answer a focused set of questions about your topic. Pressmaster uses your answers to write the article, capturing your perspective and tone throughout. Choose from three interview styles:

- **Quick** — Fast generation with no interview required
- **Adaptive** — Questions asked one at a time, each building on your previous answer (recommended for most users)
- **Conversational** — Speak directly with Pressmaster like you're talking to a journalist

**Import and repurpose** — Paste a URL, upload a file (PDF, DOC, or slides), or paste text directly. Pressmaster transforms your source material into a polished article that matches your brand voice.

## What Pressmaster does during article creation

- **Automatic data enrichment** — Pulls supporting facts and statistics from trusted sources
- **Real-time fact-checking** — Verifies claims and adds citations to strengthen credibility
- **SEO optimization** — Structures content for target keywords and readability
- **Professional formatting** — Applies consistent structure, headlines, and flow

## Create an article

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Article Writer">
    From your dashboard, click **New Article** in the top-right corner or from the main interface.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your creation method">
    Select **AI Interview** to answer questions about your topic, or choose **Import** to paste a link, upload a file, or paste text directly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your article settings">
    Set your content type (Thought Leadership, Press Release, or Company News), content focus, article length, tone, brand voice, and narrative style. Enable **Web and Citation Search** to automatically add sources and backlinks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the article strategy">
    Before writing begins, Pressmaster shows you an overview of the angle direction, topic, strategic pillars, and target tone. Approve the strategy or request changes before continuing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the interview or import your source">
    If you chose the AI Interview, answer the questions. Pressmaster adapts each question based on your previous answers. If you chose Import, paste your source material or upload your file.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the generated article">
    Once the article is ready, open it in the editor. Use highlight-and-comment, direct text editing, or the **Chat with AI** feature to refine specific sections conversationally.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your publishing destination">
    Choose where to publish: your connected blog, Pressmaster Newsroom, or the Marketplace for press release distribution.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish immediately or schedule">
    Click **Publish** to go live now, or select a date and time to schedule the article for later.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Publishing destinations

- **Connected blog** — Publish directly to WordPress, Webflow, Wix, or Ghost
- **Pressmaster Newsroom** — Add the article to your branded media hub
- **Marketplace** — Submit for press release distribution to 400+ publications

<Note>
  If you select the Marketplace as your publishing destination, the article is submitted as a press release and will not be sent to your connected blog. Choose your destination before submitting.
</Note>

## Repurpose articles into social posts

After publishing, you can turn any article into platform-specific social media posts. In the Social Suite, click **New AI Post**, then select **Use Existing Article as Reference**. Pressmaster generates native versions for each channel you select.

<Tip>
  You can turn any published article into social media posts. Select "New AI Post", choose your article, and Pressmaster creates platform-specific versions.
</Tip>
