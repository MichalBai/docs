Explore Ideas is the mode where thinking and doing live in the same place. Chat with your content library, your analytics, your interviews, and the web, develop a strategy, then hand it off to creation with a single message. No copy-pasting. No switching tools.

## When to use Explore Ideas

- You want to analyze what's working before deciding what to create next
- You have a rough idea you want to develop before committing to a format
- You want to chat with past interviews or content to find angles
- You want to explore trends or do web research before writing
- You want to create a quick post from inside a conversation

## What you can chat with

Explore Ideas has access to everything in your Pressmaster account:

- **Your content library**, all drafts, published posts, and scheduled content
- **Your analytics**, likes, impressions, engagement, top-performing posts
- **Your interviews**, every interview you've done, transcribed and searchable
- **Your pipeline**, what's currently in progress across all stages
- **The web**, run searches and pull in external context on any topic
- **Your Twin**, chat directly with your Content Twin to develop ideas from your own thinking

## How the handover works

The key difference from other chat tools: you never have to copy-paste anything out of Explore Ideas.

When you've developed ideas you want to act on, just tell Pressmaster in natural language:

> *"Looks good, plan ahead, LinkedIn and X, daily posting"*

Pressmaster automatically pulls every idea from the conversation, builds a content calendar, and moves everything into Plan Ahead mode. Your calendar gets created without you touching a single draft manually.

You can also hand off to Instant Creation for a single post, or save ideas directly to your content queue.

## In-chat publishing

Found a perfect angle and want to act on it immediately? Just say so:

> *"That idea looks perfect, create a LinkedIn post now"*

Pressmaster generates the post inside the chat. You can review it, then click **Publish** or **Schedule** directly from the conversation, no switching to the editor, no copy-pasting.

## Analyse performance and iterate

Chat with your performance data to turn numbers into decisions:

- *"Which of my posts got the most engagement last month?"*
- *"What topics should I focus on based on my analytics?"*
- *"Compare my LinkedIn vs X performance"*

Once you have a direction, hand it off to Plan Ahead or create a post immediately, all from the same session.

## Save ideas

Any idea, angle, or draft you develop in Explore Ideas can be saved to your content queue with a single message. Nothing gets lost when the session ends.

<Tip>
  You don't need to structure your messages. Just talk to Pressmaster the way you'd talk to a strategist, it figures out what to do with the context.
</Tip>
