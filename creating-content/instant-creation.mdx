Instant Creation is the fastest way to go from an idea to a finished piece of content. Drop in a topic, import existing material, or start from a trend — and Pressmaster generates publication-ready content in your voice across any format.

## Content types

- **Social media posts** — LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, Facebook, Bluesky
- **Articles** — long-form thought leadership with SEO optimization, data enrichment, and fact-checking
- **Newsletters** — native newsletter format, ready to send
- **Video scripts** — structured scripts for video content
- **Press releases** — third-person announcements distributed to 400+ publications

## Two ways to start

**Describe your idea** — type your topic, and Pressmaster uses your brand guide and voice to generate the content.

**Import and repurpose** — paste a URL, upload a file (PDF, DOC, or slides), or paste text. Pressmaster transforms your source material into a polished piece that matches your brand voice.

You can also start from an **interview**: launch a text, voice, or video interview on your topic, then generate content directly from your answers.

## Create a piece of content

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Instant Creation">
    Click **+ Create** and select **Instant Creation**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your idea or source">
    Type your topic, paste a link, upload a file, or pull a trend from Find Ideas. You can also start an interview to develop the idea first.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your content type and settings">
    Select your format, content type, length, tone, and brand voice. For articles, enable **Web and Citation Search** to automatically pull sources and backlinks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the strategy (articles)">
    For articles, Pressmaster shows you the angle, topic, strategic pillars, and tone before writing. Approve or request changes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and refine in the editor">
    All content types open in the unified editor. Use **Chat with AI**, highlight-and-comment, or direct editing to refine. The editor has full access to your Twin — so edits stay consistent with your voice without re-explaining yourself.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit across all channels simultaneously">
    If you're creating for multiple platforms, enable **Apply to All** and changes propagate instantly across every format. Or open each draft individually to fine-tune platform-specific versions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add images (optional)">
    Upload your own images or let Pressmaster generate on-brand visuals — stock images, infographics, quote visuals, or cinematic carousels (LinkedIn and Instagram).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish or schedule">
    Publish immediately or pick a date and time. Content goes directly to your connected channels, blog, or Newsroom. **Auto First Comments** can be enabled to automatically add a first comment on LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook, and other supported platforms after publishing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The unified editor

One editor handles all content types — social posts, articles, newsletters, and scripts. From the editor you can:

- **Chat with AI** to refine sections conversationally
- **SEO optimize, humanize, or improve readability** — the agent builds a multi-step plan and executes it
- **Share a feedback link** so external reviewers can comment directly on the draft
- **Publish to any destination** — social channels, blog, Newsroom, or Marketplace

## Publishing destinations

- **Social channels** — LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, Facebook, Bluesky
- **Connected blog** — WordPress, Webflow, Wix, or Ghost
- **Pressmaster Newsroom** — your branded media hub
- **Marketplace** — press release distribution to 400+ publications

## Press releases

Press releases follow a strict third-person format and are distributed through the Pressmaster Marketplace. Before submitting, ensure your release:

- Is newsworthy (product launches, funding rounds, milestones, partnerships)
- Is 300–600 words (up to 1,600 words if content remains relevant)
- Contains no promotional language or first-person narrative
- Includes a verifiable media contact name and email

Distribution typically takes 24–48 hours. A full syndication report is available in your dashboard within 24 hours of publication.

<Note>
  If you select the Marketplace as your publishing destination for an article, it will not be sent to your connected blog. Set your destination before you start writing.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Add links in comments rather than in the post body on social platforms — links in posts reduce reach due to platform algorithms.
</Tip>
