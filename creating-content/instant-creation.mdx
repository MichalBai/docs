Instant Creation is the fastest way to go from an idea to a finished piece of content. Drop in a topic, an existing piece of content, or a trend from Find Ideas — and Pressmaster generates a publication-ready article, social post, or press release in your voice.

## Content types

You can create any of the following with Instant Creation:

- **Social media posts** — LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, Facebook, Bluesky
- **Articles** — long-form thought leadership with SEO optimization and fact-checking
- **Press releases** — third-person announcements distributed to 400+ publications

## Two ways to start

**Describe your idea** — Type what you want to write about. Pressmaster uses your brand guide and voice to generate the content.

**Import and repurpose** — Paste a URL, upload a file (PDF, DOC, or slides), or paste text. Pressmaster transforms your source material into a polished piece that matches your brand voice.

## Create a piece of content

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Instant Creation">
    Click **+ Create** and select **Instant Creation**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your idea or source">
    Type your topic, paste a link, or upload a file. You can also pull a trend directly from Find Ideas — the content angle will be prefilled.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your content type and settings">
    Select your format (social post, article, or press release), content type, length, tone, brand voice, and target platform(s). Enable **Web and Citation Search** to automatically add sources and backlinks for articles.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the strategy (articles)">
    For articles, Pressmaster shows you an overview of the angle, topic, strategic pillars, and tone before writing. Approve the strategy or request changes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and refine in the editor">
    Once the content is generated, open it in the editor. Use the **Chat with AI** panel, highlight-and-comment, or direct text editing to refine any section. You can ask the editor to SEO optimize, humanize language, or improve readability.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add images (optional)">
    Upload your own images or let Pressmaster generate on-brand visuals — stock images, infographics, quote visuals, or cinematic carousels (LinkedIn and Instagram).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish or schedule">
    Publish immediately or pick a date and time. Content goes directly to your connected channels, blog, or Newsroom.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Publishing destinations

- **Social channels** — LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, Facebook, Bluesky
- **Connected blog** — WordPress, Webflow, Wix, or Ghost
- **Pressmaster Newsroom** — your branded media hub
- **Marketplace** — press release distribution to 400+ publications

## Press releases

Press releases follow a strict third-person, journalistic format and are distributed through the Pressmaster Marketplace. Before submitting, make sure your release:

- Is newsworthy (product launches, funding rounds, milestones, partnerships)
- Is 300–600 words (up to 1,600 words if the content remains clear)
- Contains no promotional language or first-person narrative
- Includes a verifiable media contact name and email

Distribution typically takes 24–48 hours after submission. A distribution report with links to every syndication is available in your dashboard within 24 hours of publication.

<Note>
  If you select the Marketplace as your publishing destination for an article, it will not be sent to your connected blog. Set your destination before you start writing.
</Note>

## Platform notes

**LinkedIn** — Pressmaster supports LinkedIn posts only. LinkedIn's API does not allow publishing LinkedIn articles through third-party tools.

**One account per platform per workspace** — Each workspace supports one connected social account per platform. Use separate workspaces for multiple accounts on the same platform.

<Tip>
  Add links in comments rather than in the post body on social platforms. Links in posts reduce reach due to platform algorithms.
</Tip>
