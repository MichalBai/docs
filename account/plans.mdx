## Switching plans

Upgrade or downgrade anytime from **Account Settings > Billing**. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

## Cancelling or pausing

To cancel or pause your subscription:

1. Go to **Account Settings > Billing**.
2. Scroll to **Danger Zone**.
3. Click **Cancel or Pause Subscription**.

<Note>
  Cancellations must be completed inside your account. Requests via email or support tickets are not accepted. You can pause for 1–3 months and your unused credits will be held until you return.
</Note>
