Find answers to the most common questions about using Pressmaster — from billing and credits to content creation and platform capabilities.

## Account & billing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need a credit card to sign up?">
    No. You can start using Pressmaster immediately without entering any payment information. You only need to add a card when you're ready to continue after your free credits are used.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I switch plans anytime?">
    Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time from **Account Settings > Billing**. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I cancel my subscription?">
    Go to **Account Settings > Billing > Danger Zone** and click **Cancel or Pause Subscription**. Cancellations must be completed from inside your account — requests via email or support tickets are not accepted.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I pause instead of cancelling?">
    Yes. You can pause your subscription for 1–3 months without losing your unused credits. Resume anytime when it fits your schedule.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Pressmaster offer refunds?">
    Pressmaster does not offer refunds for unused time or missed trial deadlines. If verified critical bugs prevented you from using the platform, contact support — exceptions may apply in those cases.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a discount for annual billing?">
    Yes. You save 25% when you pay annually on any plan.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Credits & usage

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What are credits?">
    Credits are Pressmaster's usage currency. Every AI-powered action — writing, editing, repurposing, planning, trend analysis, agent reasoning — draws from your credit balance. Non-AI features are always unlimited and never consume credits. See the [Credits](/account/credits) page for a full breakdown.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if I run out of credits?">
    Purchase additional credit packs from your billing settings, or upgrade to a higher plan. You can also enable auto top-up to prevent your credits from running out mid-project.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do credits roll over?">
    Yes. Unused credits roll over for one additional month, up to 2× your monthly plan allowance. Credits beyond that cap do not accumulate further.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Workspaces & teams

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What's the difference between Workspaces and Connected Channels?">
    Workspaces are separate environments for different clients or brands — each has its own brand guide, brand voice, content, and connected accounts. Connected Channels are the social media accounts and blog platforms you publish to from within a workspace. Workspaces are an agency feature; connected channels are available on all plans.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I manage two brands in Pressmaster?">
    Yes. Create an additional workspace for each brand from your dashboard. Each added workspace receives a 40% discount.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I post to both my personal and company social account?">
    Each workspace supports one social media account per platform. To post to both a personal and a company account on the same platform, use two separate workspaces.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I invite team members?">
    Go to **Account Settings > Members** and invite your team via email. Team member invitations are available on Agency plans.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Content creation

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What types of content can Pressmaster create?">
    Pressmaster creates long-form LinkedIn posts, Twitter/X threads, Instagram captions, Bluesky threads, Facebook posts, articles, and press releases.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can Pressmaster create emails or newsletters?">
    Not currently. Email and newsletter creation is on the roadmap.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I regenerate content I'm not happy with?">
    Yes. Go back to the content creation screen, adjust your settings or inputs, and regenerate.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What's the recommended length for press releases?">
    There is no hard limit, but aim for 300–600 words for standard press releases. The maximum recommended length is 1,600 words.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Pressmaster support citations?">
    Not yet. A built-in citation system is in development. Until then, verify facts using external tools such as Perplexity before publishing.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Pressmaster have an affiliate program?">
    Yes. Click the orange gift icon in your dashboard, copy your referral link, and earn up to $1,000 per successful signup.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
