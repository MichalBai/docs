Credits are Pressmaster's usage currency for every AI-powered action on the platform. Each plan includes a monthly credit allowance, and credits are only ever deducted for successful AI output, errors, retries, and clarifications never cost you credits.

## What credits cover

Credits are consumed by any action that requires AI reasoning or generation:

- Writing, editing, and repurposing content
- Content planning and ideation
- Trend analysis and hook simulations
- Agent-based reasoning and coordination (Agent Team)
- Twin usage, training, and chat

All non-AI features, browsing the dashboard, managing integrations, scheduling posts, are always unlimited and never consume credits.

## Agent Studio credit behavior

Agent Studio is a coordinated multi-agent system, not a single action. Each run bundles ideation generation, agent chat and reasoning, and content calendar planning across a chosen time horizon. This is why it consumes more credits than single-step features — a 30-day content calendar uses significantly more credits than a 7-day plan. Choose the planning scope that matches your current need to manage credit usage efficiently.

## Features with dynamic credit usage

Some features scale their credit draw based on the depth or size of the output:

| Feature | Credit behavior |
|---|---|
| Content generation | Charged per generated output |
| Agent content calendar | Scales with planning scope |
| Twin-guided interview | Scales with interview depth |
| Twin training | Scales with training batch size |
| Agent topic discovery | Scales with research depth |

## Credit allowances by plan

Each plan includes a fixed monthly credit allowance. Credits reset on your billing date each month.

| Plan | Monthly credits |
|---|---|
| Starter | 9,000 |
| Pro | 18,000 |
| Leader | 30,000 |
| Agency Starter | 40,000 |
| Agency Pro | 200,000 |

## Rollover

Unused credits roll over for one additional month, up to a maximum of 2× your plan's monthly allowance. On the Pro plan, for example, you can carry a maximum of 36,000 credits into any given month. Credits beyond the 2× cap do not accumulate further.

## Top-ups

If you need more credits during a billing cycle, you can purchase additional credit packs at any time. Top-ups are optional and designed for:

- Temporary spikes in activity
- Heavy campaign periods
- Agency or team workflows with elevated volume

Enable **auto top-up** in your billing settings to prevent your credits from running out mid-project. You can set a monthly cap to stay in control of your spend.

## Checking your credit balance

Your remaining credits are always visible in your dashboard. You do not need to navigate to a separate page, the balance updates in real time after each AI action.

<Note>
  Credits are deducted only for successful AI actions. If the AI returns an error or you retry a generation, no credits are consumed.
</Note>
