Pressmaster's white-label feature lets you replace all Pressmaster branding with your own — logo, favicon, banner, and a custom domain. Your clients log in at your subdomain and experience a platform that looks and feels like yours. No "powered by Pressmaster" attribution appears anywhere in their experience.

## Requirements

- Agency Starter plan or higher — white-label is an agency-only feature
- Access to your agency dashboard
- A domain you control, with access to its DNS settings

## Set up white-label branding

<Steps>
  <Step title="Switch to your agency dashboard">
    Click the workspace dropdown in the top navigation and select your agency. White-label settings are only accessible from the agency dashboard, not from individual client workspaces.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open white-label settings">
    Go to **Settings > White Label** to access all branding and domain options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your logo">
    Upload your agency logo. This replaces the Pressmaster logo throughout the platform — on every page your clients see.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a favicon">
    Upload a favicon file. This appears in the browser tab when clients access your branded platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload a banner">
    Upload a banner image. Clients see this banner whenever they log in through your custom domain.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your subdomain">
    Choose a subdomain for your branded platform — for example, `app.youragency.com` or `content.youragency.com`. Do not use your root domain (`youragency.com`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="View DNS instructions">
    Click **View Instructions** inside the domain settings panel. This shows you the exact CNAME record details you need to add at your domain provider.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a CNAME record at your domain provider">
    Log in to your domain provider (GoDaddy, Namecheap, Cloudflare, or similar) and navigate to **DNS Settings**. Add a new record with the following values:

    - **Type:** CNAME
    - **Name:** your subdomain prefix only (e.g., `app` for `app.youragency.com`)
    - **Value:** `target.pressmaster.ai`

    Save the record.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your domain in Pressmaster">
    Return to **Settings > White Label** in Pressmaster. Enter your full subdomain (e.g., `app.youragency.com`) and click **Connect Domain**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for DNS propagation">
    DNS changes typically take 30 minutes to 2 hours to propagate, depending on your provider. You'll see a success confirmation in Pressmaster once the connection is verified.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Use a subdomain (e.g., `app.youragency.com`), not your root domain (`youragency.com`). CNAME records cannot be set on root domains.
</Warning>

## What's white-labeled after setup

Once you've uploaded your logo, favicon, and banner and connected your subdomain, the full platform is branded as yours:

- All platform pages and interfaces show your logo and branding
- Interview links sent to clients display your logo, not Pressmaster's
- Clients log in at your subdomain and see your branded platform throughout their session
- No Pressmaster attribution appears anywhere in the client experience

## Benefits

- **Complete brand control** — the entire client experience reflects your agency identity
- **Professional credibility** — clients access the platform through your own domain
- **Client loyalty** — clients associate the platform with your agency, not Pressmaster
