Interview links let you send a Pressmaster AI interview directly to your clients. They answer questions in their own words, and Pressmaster transforms their responses into polished articles and content — no interview session required on your end. Your client completes the interview at their own pace from any browser, including mobile.

## How interview links work

1. You generate an interview link in Pressmaster for a specific content topic
2. Your client receives the link and completes the AI interview at a time that suits them — typically 5–10 minutes
3. Pressmaster uses their responses to generate content in their brand voice
4. You review, edit, and publish the draft from your agency dashboard

The content produced reflects exactly what your client said — their perspective, tone, and expertise — without requiring a live session or recording call.

## Generate an interview link

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start an article in HQ mode">
    Open your client's workspace and click **New Article**. Select **HQ mode** to access the full interview and strategy configuration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure interview settings">
    Set the content type, focus area, length, brand voice, and narrative style. Select **Adaptive Interview** as the interview method — this lets your client type or record audio responses for each question, and is the best option for external interviews.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and approve the strategy">
    Pressmaster generates an interview strategy showing the topic, angle, and key pillars. Review the strategy and either approve it or request adjustments before continuing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy the external interview link">
    Once the strategy is approved, click **Copy External Interview Link** to copy the shareable URL to your clipboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the link to your client">
    Share the link by email, Slack, or any messaging platform. Your client doesn't need a Pressmaster account to complete the interview.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your client completes the interview">
    Your client opens the link and answers the interview questions. They can type or record audio for each question. The interview takes approximately 5–10 minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and publish the draft">
    Once your client submits, Pressmaster generates the article using their responses. Review the draft in your dashboard, make any edits, and publish when ready.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Send interview links with a short note explaining what to expect — "This is a 5-minute AI interview. Just answer naturally; there are no right or wrong answers."
</Tip>

## Requirements

Generating external interview links requires a Pro plan or higher.

## White-label interview links

If you've set up white-label branding, interview links automatically display your agency's logo and branding — not Pressmaster's. Your client sees a fully branded interview experience from the moment they open the link.

Set up white-label branding before sending interview links to clients. See [White Label](/agencies/white-label) for setup instructions.

## Tracking interview completion

Agency Pro includes Interview Tracking, which gives you a centralized view of all interview activity across your client workspaces:

- **Pending** — the client has received the link but hasn't started yet
- **In progress** — the client is actively completing the interview
- **Completed** — the interview is finished and ready for article generation

You receive a notification when a client completes an interview. From the tracking view, click **Create Article** on any completed interview to generate the content immediately — without navigating into the individual workspace.
