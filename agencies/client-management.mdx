Pressmaster's agency plans give you dedicated workspaces for each client, team member invitations, and approval workflows — so you can manage content for multiple clients from one platform without mixing up brands or voices. Everything stays isolated per client: brand voice, connected channels, and content calendars all live in their own workspace.

## Workspaces

Each workspace is a fully isolated environment with its own brand voice, connected channels, and content history. When you switch between clients, you switch workspaces — nothing carries over between them.

| Plan | Workspaces included |
|------|---------------------|
| Agency Starter | 2 workspaces |
| Agency Pro | 10 workspaces |

Additional workspaces are available on both plans at a 40% discount. Switch between workspaces using the workspace dropdown in the top navigation of your dashboard.

<Tip>
  Create a separate workspace for each client brand — this keeps brand voices, connected channels, and content calendars fully isolated per client.
</Tip>

## Inviting team members

Team member invitations are available on Agency Starter and above. To invite a team member:

1. Go to **Settings > Team**
2. Enter the team member's email address and send the invitation
3. Once they accept, they can create and edit content within the workspaces you assign them

## Feedback and approval flow

Agency plans include a structured feedback workflow so clients can review content before it's published.

1. Share a draft article or social post with your client by clicking **Ask for Feedback** and entering their email or copying the feedback link
2. Your client opens the link in any browser — no Pressmaster account required
3. They leave inline comments on the draft, similar to Google Docs
4. You receive a notification and can review the feedback in your dashboard under the comments section
5. Use **Ask AI** to apply specific feedback directly to the draft
6. Publish the content once your client approves

Track feedback status for all clients from the agency dashboard — see which drafts are pending review, which have received comments, and which are approved and ready to publish.

## Content tracking

Agency Pro includes Interview Tracking and Content Feedback Tracking across all client workspaces. From your agency dashboard you can:

- See which clients have completed their interviews, which are in progress, and which haven't started
- Monitor approval status for all shared content across every client workspace
- Send follow-up reminders for outstanding feedback requests without leaving the dashboard

This removes the need to check each workspace individually — all activity is visible from one central view.
