The Content Library is your central hub for every piece of content in Pressmaster. All content created through Brainstorm, Instant Creation, or Plan Ahead flows here automatically — so you always have a complete view of where things stand.

## Content stages

The Content Library organizes your content into four stages:

| Stage | What it means |
|---|---|
| **Queue** | Drafts in progress, not yet submitted for review |
| **Review** | Content pending approval before publishing |
| **Scheduled** | Approved content with a set publish date and time |
| **Published** | Content that has already gone live |

Each content card shows the title, excerpt, content pillars (Growth, Trust Build, Conversion, etc.), target platforms, and scheduled date.

## Managing content

From any card in the library you can:

- **Open and edit** — make changes in the editor at any stage before publishing
- **Reschedule** — move a scheduled piece to a different date or time
- **Approve** — move content from Review to Scheduled
- **Publish immediately** — push content live without waiting for a scheduled time
- **Cancel** — remove a scheduled post before it goes out

## Auto-Repurpose

Content in your Queue can be tagged for **Auto-Repurpose** — Pressmaster will automatically turn it into additional formats or platform-specific variants when the repurpose cycle runs.

## Filtering and search

Use the search bar to find content by keyword, or apply filters to narrow by stage, platform, content pillar, or date range.

<Tip>
  Keep your Review column clear — regularly approving or adjusting content in that stage ensures your publishing schedule stays on track.
</Tip>
