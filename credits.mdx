Credits represent AI work done on your behalf. Every time Pressmaster writes, edits, repurposes, plans, analyzes trends, or runs agent reasoning, it draws from your credit balance. You never deal with tokens, compute costs, or technical limits — credits abstract all of that into one simple, predictable unit.

## What credits cover

Credits are used for any action that involves AI:

- Writing, editing, and repurposing content
- Content planning and ideation
- Trend analysis and hook simulations
- Agent-based reasoning and coordination (Supernova / Agent-Team)

Non-AI features — like browsing the dashboard, managing integrations, or scheduling posts — are always unlimited and never consume credits.

## Credit consumption by action

Credit usage is approximate and may vary slightly based on content length and complexity.

| Action | Credits (approx) |
|---|---|
| Chat with AI | ~30 / message |
| Post creation | ~90 |
| Article creation | ~150 |
| Optimize content | ~75 |
| Twin usage | ~100 |
| Twin training | ~300 / asset |
| Twin chat | ~95 |
| Hook & angle simulation | ~20 |

Some features scale based on output depth. Supernova (Agent-Team) consumes more credits than single-step actions because it bundles ideation, agent reasoning, and content calendar planning across time horizons. Twin training scales with the size of the training batch.

## Credits per plan

Each plan includes a monthly credit allowance:

| Plan | Monthly credits |
|---|---|
| Starter | 9,000 |
| Pro | 18,000 |
| Leader | 30,000 |
| Agency plans | Large shared pools |

## Rollover

Credits reset on your monthly billing date. Unused credits roll over for one additional month, up to a maximum of 2× your plan's monthly allowance. For example, on the Pro plan, you can carry a maximum of 36,000 credits into any given month.

## Top-ups

If you need more credits mid-cycle, you can purchase a top-up at any time. Top-ups are optional and are designed for heavy campaign periods, temporary spikes in activity, or larger agency and team workflows. You can also enable auto top-up to prevent interruptions, with a monthly cap to stay in control of spend.

<Note>
  You always see your remaining credits in the dashboard. Credits are deducted only for successful AI actions — errors, retries, and clarifications never consume credits.
</Note>
