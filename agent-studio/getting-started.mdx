This is where your content operation stops needing you. Build autonomous agents by describing what you want. They wake up on their own, execute tasks, monitor performance, and publish content while you're offline. Connect your entire tool stack across 3,000+ integrations. Message your agents from Slack, WhatsApp, or Telegram like you would a teammate. The longer you run it, the smarter it gets.

## Your default agent team

Your agent team is ready to use on day one. It ships with built-in research tools, winning workflows, and proven skills, no configuration required. Start getting results immediately, then customize as you go.

| Agent | Role |
|---|---|
| **Jim** | Orchestrator, runs the operation, briefs and delegates to the team, quality-checks all outputs |
| **Scout** | Finds trending topics and audience signals |
| **Strategist** | Turns briefs and context into hooks and content plans |
| **Creator** | Handles all content creation across formats |
| **Analyst** | Tracks content performance and surfaces insights |
| **Heartbeat** | Always-on standup persona that reviews your operation and flags what needs attention |

You can disable any default agent and replace it with your own. To add a custom agent, click **Add Agent** or describe what you need in chat and Pressmaster builds it for you.

## Building agents via chat

Create, train, and deploy agents by chatting with them. Describe what you want an agent to do, and Pressmaster builds it. Adjust its behavior, give it new skills, and deploy, all through conversation.

You can also send your agent a **voice note** directly, describe a task out loud and the agent picks it up and executes.

## Communicating with your agents

Chat with your agents from wherever you are:

- **In-app**, directly within Pressmaster
- **Slack**, your agent appears as **Superagent** in your workspace; send messages and get updates like any teammate
- **Telegram**, delegate tasks from your phone; your agent responds as **Superagent**
- **WhatsApp**, send instructions and receive results on mobile
- **Email**, loop agents into your existing workflows
- **Voice notes**, send a voice message and your agent executes the task

Context is maintained across all channels. Delegate from anywhere, text your agent team like you would a colleague.

## Team workspaces

Every member of your team gets their own agent. Each agent is tied to that person's identity, content style, and goals, so your entire team can run autonomously, with each agent working independently for the right person.

## How credits work

Agent Studio uses a **25% markup** on the underlying AI model costs. You pay for what your agents actually use, no flat monthly fee for compute. You can monitor usage and set spending limits per workspace from the account settings.

## What agents can do for you

- Create drafts and manage your Content Library
- Review analytics and surface performance insights
- Publish content to connected channels
- Monitor industry trends and competitor activity
- Execute research tasks and store findings in the Artifact Library
- Run background tasks on a schedule, without you logging in
- Connect your note-taker (Granola, etc.) and automatically extract insights from your meetings
- Send you content links via WhatsApp, Slack, or Telegram for review without you opening the app
- Fire interview links on a schedule, research the topic, send the link, handle the follow-up
- Push results where they belong: content to your library, leads to your CRM, updates to your inbox

## Agent uses Pressmaster for you

Your agent has full access to the Pressmaster UI. It doesn't just produce outputs in a chat, it actively manages your content library, moves cards between stages, creates drafts, and fires creation flows on your behalf. You set the rules, the agent does the work.

## Parallel task execution

Agents can run many tasks simultaneously. Spin off 10, 50, or 100 tasks and the agent executes them in parallel, no queue, no waiting.

## Model routing

Agents automatically use the most powerful models for complex tasks and cheaper models for simple ones. You never have to configure this, routing happens automatically to maximize quality while keeping costs low.

## Integration connector

All tool integrations run through **Pipedream**, which is owned by Workday and operates with enterprise-grade security. Custom code, custom scripts, and custom tool creation are intentionally disabled in the agent to keep it isolated and secure.
