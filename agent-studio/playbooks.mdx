Playbooks are pre-built, ready-to-deploy agent systems for specific use cases. Instead of building an agent workflow from scratch, browse the Playbook Library, find the system that matches your goal, click deploy, and it's running.

## Available playbooks

- **SEO Playbook**, keyword research, content drafting, publishing, and backlink outreach, fully automated
- **Reddit Playbook**, agent participates in relevant Reddit conversations using your Twin and brand voice
- **GTM Sales Playbook**, outreach sequences, lead research, and follow-up workflows
- **AI GEO Optimizer**, optimizes your content for AI-powered search (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude)
- **Podcast Pitching Playbook**, finds relevant podcasts, drafts pitches using your profile, sends via your email

<Note>
  More playbooks are coming soon.
</Note>

## How it works

1. Browse the Playbook Library inside Agent Studio
2. Click **Deploy** on any playbook
3. Connect the required tools (e.g., your email, your website, your social accounts)
4. The agent system is live, no configuration needed

You can iterate on any playbook after deploying, adjust skills, change frequency, add custom instructions.

## Build your own playbooks

Save your own agent configurations as reusable playbooks, shareable internally across your team, or deployed for every new client as part of your agency's standard setup.
