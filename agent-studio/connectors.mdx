Connectors give your agents access to external tools and platforms. Once connected, your agents can pull data, send messages, run research, and push results without you having to move information manually.

## Communication channels

Connect your preferred messaging app so you can communicate with your agents from anywhere — and receive notifications, interview links, and updates directly in the app you already use.

| Channel | Best for |
|---|---|
| **Slack** | Teams and business communication |
| **Telegram** | Personal use and on-the-go messaging |
| **WhatsApp** | Personal use if it's your main messaging app |

**Pick the one you already use most.** The goal is to remove friction — the more natural the channel feels, the more consistently you'll use it. Once connected, you can send your agent a quick voice note, save an idea, or check in on what's running, all without opening the web app.

## Tool integrations

Connect the tools you use daily so your agents can access them as part of automated workflows. Go to **Agent Studio > Connectors** and search for the tool you want to add.

### Research and SEO

- **Ahrefs** — keyword research, backlink analysis, and SEO workflows
- **Google Search Console** — track rankings, index coverage, and search performance
- **Google Analytics** — connect traffic data to inform content strategy

### Meetings and notes

- **Fathom** — pull meeting recordings and AI summaries into content workflows
- **Fireflies** — same as Fathom; use whichever you already have

### CRM and outreach

- **Gmail** — send interview links, outreach, or updates from your inbox via the agent
- Connect your CRM to push leads, contacts, and research results directly from agent outputs

### Social and content

- **Reddit** — research trending threads and replies in your niche
- **X (Twitter)** — monitor conversations and content performance

## Setting up a connector

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Agent Studio > Connectors">
    Open **Agent Studio** from the left sidebar and click **Connectors**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search for your tool">
    Use the search bar to find the tool or channel you want to connect.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect and follow the steps">
    Each connector has its own authentication flow. Follow the on-screen instructions. If you run into issues, reach out to support or check the help docs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tell your agent to use it">
    Once connected, reference the tool in your agent instructions. For example: "Set up a weekly workflow using my Fathom integration." Your agent will know it has access and use it accordingly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Start with your communication channel first — that's what unlocks the full hands-free experience. Add tool integrations as you discover what your agent needs to do the work you have in mind.
</Tip>
