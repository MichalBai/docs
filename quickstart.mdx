---
title: "Set up Pressmaster and publish your first piece of content"
description: "Set up your brand voice, connect your channels, and publish your first piece of content — from account creation to live post in under 10 minutes."
---

This guide walks you through everything you need to go from a blank account to publishing real content. By the end, Pressmaster knows your brand, your voice, and where to publish — so every piece of content it creates sounds authentically like you.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to [pressmaster.ai](https://www.pressmaster.ai) and sign up. No credit card is required to get started — your free account includes credits to experience the full platform.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Run the Brand Interview">
    The Brand Interview teaches Pressmaster who you are. Go to **Settings > Brand Guides** and choose one of three options:

    - Paste your website URL — Pressmaster reads it and builds your brand guide automatically.
    - Answer a few questions about your mission, target audience, and tone.
    - Upload a detailed brand guide document for the most personalized results.

    You don't need to answer every question. Share what matters most, then save. Pressmaster builds your brand guide in about 45 seconds and applies it to all future content.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Define your Brand Voice">
    Click **Add Brand Voice** to teach Pressmaster how you actually write. Give your voice a name (for example, "LinkedIn" or "Articles"), then choose a training method:

    - **Paste content you've written** — copy in a post, article, or email that sounds like you.
    - **Drop a link** to a piece of content that captures your style.

    Click **Start Analyzing**. Pressmaster reads your tone, sentence structure, rhythm, and narrative patterns. Once the analysis completes, review the results and adjust anything that doesn't fit. Click **Apply Brand Voice** when you're satisfied.

    For best results, create two brand voices — one for social media posts and one for long-form articles.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect your channels">
    Go to **Settings > Integrations** and connect the social media accounts you want to publish to. Click **Connect** next to each platform and complete the authentication flow. Pressmaster supports LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, Facebook, and Bluesky.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect your blog (optional)">
    If you have a blog, flip the blog switcher on in **Settings > Integrations**. Choose your platform — WordPress, Ghost, Wix, Webflow, Blogger, or Medium — and connect it in seconds. Once connected, Pressmaster can publish articles directly to your blog.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set up TrendMaster">
    Click **Set Preferences** in TrendMaster to tell Pressmaster what topics matter to you. Select your industry, region, language, expertise level, and any specific keywords you want to track. Then click **Start** and **Apply Preferences**.

    TrendMaster will begin surfacing trending topics in your space, filtered through your brand, so you always have relevant ideas ready.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create your first content">
    You're ready to publish. Choose how you want to start:

    - **5-minute interview** — Answer a few questions about a topic and Pressmaster generates a full batch of content from your answers.
    - **Agent-Team (Supernova)** — Let the multi-agent system plan, write, and schedule weeks of content autonomously.

    Review the generated content, select your brand voice from the dropdown, and publish or schedule it to your connected channels.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Start with a 5-minute interview about a topic you're passionate about — it helps the AI understand your voice while creating your first batch of content.
</Tip>
