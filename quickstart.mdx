This guide walks you through the five setup steps that get Pressmaster working at full capacity. Each step unlocks credits you keep — and by the end, the platform knows your brand, your voice, and where to publish.

## Setting up your account

### 1. Connect your social channels
### +200 credits

Go to **Settings > Integrations** and connect the social media accounts you want to publish to. Click **Connect** next to each platform and complete the authentication flow. Pressmaster supports LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, Facebook, and Bluesky.

If you have a blog, connect it here too — WordPress, Ghost, Wix, Webflow, Blogger, or Medium. Once connected, Pressmaster can publish articles directly.

### 2. Set up your identity
### +200 credits

Go to [IdentityOS](/knowledge/identityos) to build the foundation of how Pressmaster understands you. There are three things to set up:

- **Brand Guide** — paste your website URL or answer a few questions about your mission, audience, and tone. Pressmaster builds your brand guide in about 45 seconds.
- **Brand Voice** — paste a piece of content you've written. Pressmaster analyzes your tone, structure, and rhythm, then applies it to everything it creates for you. Create one voice for social posts and one for long-form content for best results.
- **Content Twin** — your Twin is created automatically, but you'll train it more deeply in step 4.

### 3. Activate your Super Agent
### +1,000 credits

Go to [Agent Studio](/agent-studio/overview) and activate your Super Agent. Once active, it runs in the background — monitoring your niche, planning content, and executing tasks autonomously so you don't have to manage every step manually.

### 4. Train your Twin and reach 100 IQ
### +1,000 credits

Go to [IdentityOS](/knowledge/identityos) and run your first training sessions. Your Content Twin maps your thinking — your stories, frameworks, opinions, and expertise — so Pressmaster creates content that genuinely sounds like you.

Complete any of the guided trainings (Identity & Values, Story & Purpose, Expertise & Knowledge) or upload existing content you've written. Aim for a **Twin IQ Score of 100 or above** — that's the threshold where the Twin is reliable enough to generate accurate, on-brand content without you needing to review every word.

### 5. Publish your first 7 content pieces
### +1,500 credits

You're ready to create. Choose your starting point from the **+ Create** menu:

- **Instant Creation** — drop in an idea and get a ready-to-post article or social post in minutes
- **Brainstorm** — explore a topic through chat or interview, then turn it into content
- **Plan Ahead** — let Pressmaster build a full content plan and create multiple pieces at once

All content you create lands in your [Content Library](/content-library/overview). Review, approve, and publish — or schedule it to go out automatically.

## Your quick wins

Once your account is set up, these are the highest-value things to do first:

**Create your first content series**
Use Plan Ahead to generate a series of posts and publish them across all channels automatically.

**Turn your knowledge into high-performing content**
Run one interview in Brainstorm — Pressmaster turns your answers into a full week of posts.

**Stay ahead of trends in your industry**
Open [Find Ideas](/find-ideas/overview) and set your preferences. Pressmaster monitors your niche 24/7 so you never miss what's relevant.

**Let your agent work while you sleep**
Once your Super Agent is active, it handles content work in the background — you set it up once and it runs on its own.
