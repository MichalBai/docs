Your Pressmaster Newsroom transforms your website into a professional media hub that journalists can visit, subscribe to, and reference. It centralizes your press releases, articles, press kit, and media contacts in one branded destination — all automatically populated as you publish content.

## Why a newsroom matters

A dedicated newsroom does more than collect your content in one place. It signals to journalists, partners, and prospects that your company is established, media-ready, and worth covering.

- **Builds instant authority** — premium, conversion-optimized templates give your Newsroom a polished first impression
- **Attracts journalist attention** — a professional media kit gives reporters everything they need to write about you without chasing you for assets
- **Converts visitors into leads** — smart subscription forms capture reader email addresses directly from your Newsroom
- **Automatic Google News indexing** — every piece of content you publish is eligible for Google News distribution
- **Showcases your expertise 24/7** — automated content organization means your Newsroom is always up to date, even when you're not actively managing it

## What the Newsroom includes

| Feature | Details |
|---|---|
| Published content | All your articles and press releases, organized automatically |
| Press kit | Company info, logos, executive bios, and media contacts |
| Subscription forms | Capture reader emails and convert visitors into leads |
| Custom domain | Connect your own domain (e.g., `news.yourcompany.com`) |
| Drag-and-drop design | Mobile-responsive, no-code layout editor |
| Custom branding | Fonts, colors, logo, and layout options |
| Media library | Integrated storage for images and brand assets |

## SEO benefits

Your Newsroom is built for search visibility from day one.

- **Automatic Google News indexing** — all published content is submitted to Google News
- **SEO optimization** — meta titles and descriptions are configurable for every page
- **Real-time performance tracking** — built-in analytics show which content drives the most traffic
- **Content strategy insights** — audience engagement data informs what to create next
