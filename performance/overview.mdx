The Performance page gives you a full view of how your content is performing across connected channels. Use it to understand what's working, where your audience is growing, and how your brand authority is trending over time.

## Key metrics

| Metric | What it measures |
|---|---|
| **Impressions** | Total number of times your content has been seen |
| **Engagement Rate** | Percentage of viewers who interacted with your content |
| **Brand Popularity Index (BPI)** | Pressmaster's composite score of your overall brand authority and content performance |
| **Total Followers** | Combined follower count across all connected channels |

Each metric shows the current value alongside the percentage change from the previous week, so you can quickly see whether you're trending up or down.

## Brand Popularity Index (BPI)

Your BPI is a single score that captures the overall health and momentum of your brand presence. It factors in reach, engagement, consistency, and audience growth. A rising BPI means your content strategy is working — a drop is a signal to revisit your topics, cadence, or channels.

## Weekly report

Pressmaster generates a **Weekly Report** summarizing your performance across the period. The report is surfaced on your Dashboard at the start of each week and gives you a prioritized view of what to focus on next.

<Tip>
  If your BPI drops, check which channel saw the biggest engagement decline — that's usually the clearest signal of where to focus your next batch of content.
</Tip>
