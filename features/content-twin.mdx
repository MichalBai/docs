Your Content Twin is an evolving map of how you think — your ideas, stories, values, patterns, and lived experience. The Agent-Team uses it to create content that genuinely sounds like you, drawing on your real reasoning and expertise rather than generic patterns. The Twin gets sharper with every training session, upload, and interaction.

## Explore your Twin Map

Your Twin is visualized as an interactive map built around your identity. It shows how your thoughts connect across categories including:

- Stories & Anecdotes
- Frameworks & Mental Models
- Decision Patterns
- Hooks & Content Seeds
- Expertise & Domains
- Values & Principles
- Reasoning Style
- Identity & Emotion
- Proof Points & Evidence
- Vision & Predictions
- Relationships & Influences
- Opinions & Contrarian Takes
- Gaps & Tensions
- Problems & Solutions

You can zoom, scroll, and click through nodes to explore how the Twin understands your thinking. To remove a specific connection, click directly on it and delete it.

## Ways to train your Twin

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Guided trainings" icon="graduation-cap">
    Structured, topic-specific interviews that strengthen different dimensions of your identity and expertise. Available sessions include Basic Interview, Identity & Values, Story & Purpose, Expertise & Knowledge, Communication & Voice, and Leadership & Execution. Complete them in any order.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Upload material" icon="upload">
    Paste plain text, notes, articles, or research directly into the Twin. Upload files (PDF, DOC, slides) or add links — Pressmaster auto-scrapes linked pages. The Twin extracts insights and adds them to your knowledge map.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Twin Chat" icon="message-circle">
    Use the Twin's dedicated chat to explore ideas, test its reasoning, expand existing insights, and train it through conversation. Generate content seeds or examine how the Twin understands you. To make the Twin retain what you say in chat, explicitly tell it: "Memorize this."
  </Card>
  <Card title="Streaming Training" icon="zap">
    Enable Streaming Training in **Settings** to let the Twin automatically learn from your interviews, journals, and agent conversations. When disabled, the Twin learns only from what you manually upload or explicitly ask it to remember.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Twin IQ Score

Your **Twin IQ Score** measures how well-trained and reliable your Twin has become. The score ranges from 0 to 999 — the higher it is, the better your Twin performs when generating content.

| Range | Level |
|---|---|
| 0–49 | Low |
| 50–99 | Average |
| 100–149 | Good |
| 150–200 | Excellent |

You need a score of **100 or above** to use your Twin reliably for content creation. Below that threshold, the Twin does not yet have enough depth to produce accurate or authentic content based on your thinking. There is no upper limit on training — the more data you add, the higher and more precise your score becomes.

## Using your Twin in content creation

Whenever you create content, enable your Twin by clicking the **brain icon** next to the Agent. When active, the Twin makes your voice more precise, your opinions consistent, and your expertise clear — replacing generic AI output with your real thinking.

<Note>
  To make the Twin memorize something said in chat, explicitly tell it: "Memorize this." Otherwise it treats the message as temporary conversation.
</Note>
