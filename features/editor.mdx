Pressmaster's built-in editor lets you review, refine, and optimize every piece of content before publishing. Use AI-powered tools directly within the editor to adjust tone, rephrase sections, or optimize for performance — without leaving the platform.

## Key editor capabilities

**AI-assisted editing**

Select any text and click the AI button to rephrase, rewrite, or get suggestions from your AI assistant. The result inserts instantly. You can also open the **Chat with AI** panel to refine your article through conversation — ask questions or request changes, and the agent edits your draft iteratively without regenerating the whole piece.

**Optimization tools**

Ask the chat agent to SEO optimize your content, humanize the language, improve readability, or identify gaps. The agent creates a multi-step plan and executes it automatically. You can review and approve changes manually or enable auto-accept to let the agent apply everything without interruption.

**Images and media**

Click the image box at the top of the editor to upload a cover image. To insert images within the body, highlight the target location and select **Image**, or paste an image directly into the editor. All images carry through to your final published post.

**Real-time preview**

Review how your content looks before publishing, including all text, formatting, and media.

## Sharing drafts for feedback

Generate a **Feedback Link** to share your draft with colleagues, clients, or partners. Reviewers can highlight specific text and leave comments directly on the draft. Agency users receive a white-labeled version of the feedback link for client-facing collaboration.

## Publishing from the editor

Once you finish editing, publish your content directly to your connected destinations — social channels, your blog, or your Newsroom. Choose to schedule for a specific time or publish immediately. Pressmaster handles all formatting and delivery.

<Warning>
  If you choose Marketplace as your publishing destination, the article will not go to your blog. Set your destination before you start writing.
</Warning>

<Tip>
  To change the tone of an article, select the text you want to adjust and use the AI bar to rephrase or set a new tone on the spot.
</Tip>
