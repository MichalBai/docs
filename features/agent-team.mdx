Supernova is Pressmaster's multi-agent system that plans, writes, and schedules content on your behalf. Two modes determine how it thinks and what data it uses — choose the one that matches your starting point, and the Agent handles the rest.

<Warning>
  Set up both your Advanced Brand Guide and Advanced Brand Voice before running the Agent-Team. This significantly improves output quality.
</Warning>

## Two modes

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Repurpose & Series">
    Use this mode when you already have existing content and want the Agent to break it down into a structured series.

    **What you can add:**

    - Files (PDF, DOC, slides, notes)
    - Links
    - YouTube videos
    - Raw text
    - Voice notes

    **What the Agent does:**

    - Extracts the strongest ideas from your material
    - Identifies key angles and repeating patterns
    - Converts everything into multiple posts or a full content series
    - Rewrites all content in your Brand Voice
    - Produces clean, tight, high-performing structure

    This mode is input-dependent. The Agent focuses on your material, not your broader ecosystem.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Plan & Create">
    Use this mode when you want the Agent to act as your strategist and build a content plan from scratch.

    **What the Agent draws on:**

    - Your Audience & Positioning
    - Your Pinned Content
    - Your Twin
    - Your Trends
    - Your Recent Performance Signals

    **What the Agent does:**

    - Generates a calibrated 7–30 day content plan
    - Mixes Growth, Trust, and Conversion angles
    - Creates original posts based on your identity and market movement
    - Ensures narrative consistency across all formats

    This mode is ecosystem-dependent. The Agent uses everything it knows about you, your audience, and your trends.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## The Agent-Team workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your mode">
    Select **Repurpose & Series** to work from existing content, or **Plan & Create** to let the Agent build a strategy from your ecosystem data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review content ideas">
    The Agent generates structured content ideas based on your brand, voice, and selected mode. From here you can:

    - **Iterate via chat** — ask the Agent to expand, sharpen, adjust tone, or explore alternatives
    - **Select specific topics** — highlight ideas to use as anchors for the next steps
    - **Approve all** — move forward with the full set if you're satisfied with the direction
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the calendar">
    The Agent generates a proposed content schedule based on your approved ideas. Open each content piece to review its topic, angle, positioning, and purpose. You can adjust Brand Voice, format, tone, length, and narrative on a per-item basis before moving on.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the interview">
    Choose how the Agent collects your insights for each planned topic:

    - **Text interview** — answer short, focused questions directly in chat
    - **Voice interview** — speak naturally while the Agent transcribes and extracts your best ideas
    - **Skip** — if your Content Twin is well-trained, skip entirely and let the Agent generate content from your existing knowledge base
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and publish">
    Once the flow is complete, all content appears in two places:

    - **Your Calendar** — scheduled according to the proposed plan
    - **Your Feed** — unscheduled drafts ready for review

    From here you can approve, edit, reschedule, or publish any piece instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  If your Content Twin has a score above 100, you can skip the interview step entirely — the Agent-Team will generate content from your existing knowledge base.
</Tip>
