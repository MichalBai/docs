Pressmaster can automatically generate on-brand images for your articles, social media posts, and press releases — eliminating the need to source or create visuals manually. Images are generated directly from your content, using your topic, structure, and intent rather than generic prompts.

## Supported image types

| Format | Available for |
|---|---|
| Stock images | Articles, social posts |
| Infographics | Articles, social posts |
| Quote visuals | Articles, social posts |
| Cinematic carousels | LinkedIn and Instagram only |

**Cinematic carousels** are narrative-driven, multi-slide visuals designed for high engagement. **Infographics** present structured visual explanations for complex ideas and frameworks. **Quote visuals** are generated from your own insights. **Stock images** are context-aware and aligned with your topic, angle, and funnel stage.

## How image generation works

When you create a new article or social post, Pressmaster automatically suggests a creative concept based on your topic, context, and intent. All generated images are:

- **Context aware** — based on your actual content, not isolated prompts
- **Engagement optimized** — designed to increase visibility, interaction, and retention
- **Brand aware** — informed by your brand voice and positioning signals
- **Automatic** — no design tools, briefs, or manual iteration required

You can select a creative concept manually or let Pressmaster choose one automatically. If you want a different result, regenerate the image or switch to a different creative concept to explore alternative visual directions. All generated images can be downloaded and reused across your channels.

## Image generation and credits

Generating images uses Pressmaster credits. See the [credits page](/account/credits) for approximate usage per image type.

<Note>
  Adding images to press releases is recommended — you can choose from Pressmaster's stock gallery or upload your own high-quality images to make your story stand out to journalists and editors.
</Note>
