TrendMaster is your personal trend engine inside Pressmaster. It scans 500,000+ sources daily to identify emerging topics in your industry before they become mainstream — giving you 3–4 weeks of lead time to publish content while the conversation is still building.

## What TrendMaster does

- Spots emerging trends 3–4 weeks before they peak
- Delivers ready-to-use content angles customized for your expertise and brand
- Provides viral-optimized hooks based on analysis of 50,000+ successful posts
- Categorizes signals into **Trending** (real-time topics gaining traction) and **Relevant** (important, evergreen, or under-the-radar topics in your space)

Each signal includes a topic summary, sentiment analysis, and personalized content ideas tailored to your industry and voice.

## Setting up TrendMaster

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Set Preferences">
    Click **Set Preferences** in the TrendMaster section to begin configuration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your industry">
    Select up to three industries. Use the "Help me choose the right industry" feature for automated suggestions based on your expertise. Pick one or two industries that best reflect your focus — broader categories give you more discovery.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your region and language">
    Choose your geographic focus (Worldwide, North America, Europe, or a specific country) and your preferred language. North America and Global regions receive the highest news volume, giving you more signals to work with.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add expertise keywords">
    Enter keywords that reflect your specific expertise areas — for example, "AI marketing" or "leadership." Avoid overly narrow terms; broader keywords lead to better discovery. Leave the exclusions field empty unless you have specific topics to block.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply Preferences">
    Click **Apply Preferences** to activate TrendMaster. Allow 30–40 minutes for the initial trend analysis to complete. If no trending topics appear immediately, relevant signals will still be available — there is always content to work with.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Update frequency by plan

| Plan | Trending topics | Relevant topics |
|---|---|---|
| Starter | Weekly | Weekly |
| Pro | Daily | Weekly |
| Leader | Daily (real-time) | Daily |

## How to use trends to create content

Click any trending topic or relevant signal to see its summary, sentiment, and personalized content angles. Select the angle that fits your voice, then click **Create** to start writing. You can create:

- **An article** — in Quick Mode, Adaptive (written interview), or Conversational Mode (voice interview)
- **A social media post** — with the content idea automatically prefilled for your platform

You can also use any trend as input for the Agent-Team's Plan & Create mode, feeding it directly into your broader content strategy.

<Tip>
  On the Pro plan, trending topics update daily and relevant topics update weekly. Trends depend on actual news volume and may not appear every day — if your feed looks empty, check back the following day or adjust your region and keyword settings.
</Tip>
