Pin Your Thoughts lets you capture ideas the moment they strike — whether you're on your commute, in a meeting, or reading an article. Pinned content becomes raw material for your Content Twin and the Agent-Team, turning everyday inspiration into a steady pipeline of on-brand content.

## What you can pin

- **YouTube videos** — pin video URLs and summarize them for content repurposing
- **Articles and links** — save any web URL; Pressmaster generates a concise, intelligent summary
- **Podcasts** — pin podcast links and extract key ideas with a focused summary
- **Files and images** — upload documents or images directly
- **Text notes** — jot down insights, quotes, or meeting highlights

When adding a link, you can provide an optional focus note to tell Pressmaster what angle or section to pay attention to. Leave it blank for a general summary.

## How pinned content is used

Once you have a summary, Pressmaster generates **15 personalized content angles** based on your pinned content and brand guide. From any of those ideas you can immediately create:

- An article
- A social media post

All content is prefilled and editable, tailored to your tone. Every pin is saved to your **Repurpose Library**, where you can combine multiple pins, merge a pin with a past interview, or cross-reference with recently generated posts.

Pinned content also feeds into your Content Twin's knowledge base and serves as source material for the Agent-Team's Plan & Create mode.

| Plan | Pin limit |

## Using the mobile app

Download the Pressmaster mobile app to pin content from anywhere without opening a laptop. Use the app to save links, take screenshots, and summarize content on the go — everything syncs and is ready for content creation whenever you are.

<Tip>
  Use the mobile app to pin voice notes and links during your commute. Pressmaster transcribes and stores them automatically, ready to become content.
</Tip>
