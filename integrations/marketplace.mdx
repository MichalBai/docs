The Pressmaster Marketplace distributes your press releases to 400+ trusted publications, with an average of 300+ syndications per release and guaranteed placement in top-tier outlets including Business Insider and Yahoo Finance.

## Distribution overview

- **Guaranteed media coverage** once your press release is approved and distributed
- **Average 300+ pick-ups** including top-tier magazines and news sites
- **Distribution typically takes 24–48 hours** after submission
- **Appears in Google Search and Google News** through syndication to indexed publications
- **Indexed by major AI models** — publications like Business Insider and Yahoo Finance have high domain authority, meaning your release gets referenced by AI tools including ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity

## How to submit a press release

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your press release">
    From **+ Create**, select **Instant Creation** and choose **Press Release** as your content type. Use the AI editor to generate your release via Quick mode or a guided interview. Press releases must be written in third-person — this is a strict distribution requirement.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add images or videos (optional but recommended)">
    Add visuals to make your release more engaging and increase pick-up rates. Choose from the stock gallery or upload your own images and videos.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add media contact information">
    Fill out the media contact section with a name and email address. This is required by publishers and magazines for verification and follow-up.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review against distribution guidelines">
    Check your press release against the guidelines below before submitting. Releases that don't meet the standards will be rejected, which delays distribution.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your release date">
    Choose **Instant Publishing** to distribute immediately, or set a specific date and time to schedule your release.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your distribution package and submit">
    Go to **Publish > Press Distribution Marketplace**. Select your distribution package and any add-ons (guaranteed Business Insider placement, AP News, or international distribution for UK, Asia, or Australia). Complete payment and submit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Distribution guidelines

Your press release must meet the following standards to be accepted:

- **Must be newsworthy** — product launches, funding rounds, partnerships, company milestones, or awards qualify; purely promotional content does not
- **Recommended length: 300–600 words** — up to 1,600 words accepted if the content remains relevant
- **No promotional or sales language** — avoid phrases like "FREE", "Don't miss this!", or anything that reads like an advertisement
- **Must be written in third-person** — first-person and second-person narratives are not accepted
- **Must include factual, verifiable information** — unsubstantiated claims or financial promises are prohibited
- **Complete media contact required** — include a verifiable name and email address
- **No prohibited content** — personal attacks, defamatory content, or deceptive content are automatically disqualified

## After distribution

- A distribution report is available in your dashboard within 24 hours (Monday–Friday) after your release goes out
- The report includes links to every syndication placement
- You receive an email notification when the report is ready
