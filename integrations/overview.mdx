Pressmaster connects to over 1,200 tools and integrations across every category — social media, CRM, email, research, publishing platforms, and more. Connections are configurable without coding, and once set up, your agents can operate them autonomously on your behalf.

## What you can connect

- **Social media** — LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, Facebook, Bluesky
- **Blogs & publishing** — WordPress, Webflow, Wix, Ghost
- **CRM** — connect your customer data to inform content strategy
- **Email** — loop agents into email workflows
- **Research tools** — give agents access to data sources and search tools
- **And 1,200+ more** — any tool in your stack can be connected and operated by your agent team

## How it works

Integrations are available to both you and your agents. Once connected:

- **You** can publish content directly to connected platforms from the editor
- **Your agents** can operate those tools autonomously — posting, researching, updating records — without you being in the app

No engineering help required. Connect your tool stack and your agents can do anything those tools can do.

## Setting up integrations

Go to **Settings > Integrations** to connect your accounts. Each platform has a simple authentication flow — click **Connect**, complete the steps, and it's live.

For agent-specific tool integrations (CRM, research, email, and the broader 1,200+ library), configure them inside [Agent Studio](/agent-studio/getting-started).
