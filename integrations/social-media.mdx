Connecting your social media accounts to Pressmaster lets you publish and schedule content directly from the platform — no copy-pasting, no manual posting. Pressmaster supports LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, Facebook, and Bluesky.

## Supported platforms

- LinkedIn
- Twitter/X
- Instagram
- Facebook
- Bluesky

## Connect an account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings">
    Navigate to **Settings** in your Pressmaster dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Integrations">
    Click **Integrations** to see all available platforms.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a platform">
    Click on the platform you want to connect.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize Pressmaster">
    You'll be redirected to that platform's authorization page. Log in and grant Pressmaster access.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Return to Pressmaster">
    After authorizing, you'll be redirected back to Pressmaster. Your account is now connected and ready to publish to.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connecting Bluesky

Bluesky requires one extra step before the redirect. When you click the Bluesky option, enter your Bluesky handle (nickname) first, then complete the authorization on the Bluesky page.

## Account limits

Each workspace supports one connected account per platform. If you manage multiple brands or need separate accounts, create additional workspaces — each additional workspace receives a 40% discount.

## Connected channels by plan

| Plan | Connected channels |
|---|---|
| Starter | 2 channels |
| Pro | 4 channels |
| Leader | Unlimited |

<Note>
  Pressmaster supports LinkedIn posts but not LinkedIn articles — LinkedIn's API does not allow publishing articles. You can still publish standard LinkedIn posts.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Add links in comments rather than in the post body. Links in posts often reduce reach due to platform algorithms.
</Tip>
