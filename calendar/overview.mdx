The Calendar gives you a monthly view of your content schedule, every post, article, and press release that's been approved and queued for publishing, laid out by date.

All content management, reviewing drafts, moving content through stages, and approvals, happens in the [Content Library](/content-library/overview). The Calendar is your at-a-glance view of what's going out and when.

![Calendar showing monthly content schedule with scheduled and approval pending posts](/images/calendar.png)

## What the Calendar shows

All content scheduled through any creation flow, Instant Creation, Plan Ahead, or manually scheduled from the Content Library, appears on the Calendar automatically. Each item shows the content title, target platform(s), and publish time.

## Managing scheduled content

From the Calendar you can:

- **Open any item**, click a scheduled piece to review or edit it before it publishes
- **Reschedule**, drag an item to a new date, or open it and select a new time
- **Cancel a scheduled post**, open the item and click **Cancel Post** to remove it from the schedule

<Tip>
  Use the Calendar to spot gaps in your schedule at a glance. For moving content through review and approval stages, switch to the Content Library's kanban view.
</Tip>
