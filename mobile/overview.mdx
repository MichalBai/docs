The Pressmaster mobile app keeps you productive between sessions — capture ideas the moment they strike, receive real-time trend alerts, and review or publish content from anywhere. It's available for iOS and Android, and everything syncs instantly with your Pressmaster desktop account.

## What you can do on mobile

- **Capture ideas** — tap the record button and dictate your thoughts. Pressmaster transcribes the voice note automatically and adds it to your pins.
- **Pin content** — save links (YouTube videos, articles), text notes, or photos of handwritten notes to your library for later use in content creation.
- **Receive trend alerts** — get push notifications when new trending topics appear in your TrendMaster feed so you can act before competitors do.
- **Review drafts** — read and approve content your Agent-Team has created while you're away from your desk.
- **Publish content** — publish or schedule posts directly from your phone without opening a browser.
- **Import content** — take a photo of handwritten notes or import files to transform them into articles or social posts.

## Pin your thoughts on mobile

The mobile app is the primary tool for the Pin Your Thoughts feature. Tap the pin icon to capture a voice note, link, or text instantly — no formatting required. Pressmaster transcribes voice notes and summarizes pinned links automatically, then syncs everything to your Library where it's available for content creation.

To use a pinned item as the basis for a social post or article, open your Library in the web app or mobile app, select the pin, and click **Create social media post** or use it as a source in the article flow.

## Get started with the mobile app

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the app">
    Get the Pressmaster app from the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/do/app/pressmaster-ai/id6743165691?l=en-GB) for iOS or [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=ai.pressmaster.app) for Android.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in">
    Sign in with your existing Pressmaster account credentials. Your workspaces, content, and settings load automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Allow permissions">
    When prompted, allow microphone access (for voice recording) and notification access (for trend alerts). Both are needed for full functionality.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Capture your first idea">
    Tap the pin icon and record a voice note, paste a link, or type a quick thought. It will appear in your Library on both mobile and desktop.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your TrendMaster alerts">
    Open the TrendMaster section to see what's trending in your industry, or wait for a push notification to bring a new trend directly to you.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Enable push notifications so you get alerted the moment a new trending topic appears in your industry — this gives you the lead time to create content before competitors.
</Tip>

<Note>
  All plans include access to the mobile app. Content created on mobile syncs instantly with your Pressmaster desktop account.
</Note>
